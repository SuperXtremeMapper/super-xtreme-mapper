What is SXM?

Super Xtreme Mapper is a native macOS application for creating and editing Traktor Pro TSI mapping files.

Whether you're building custom controller mappings from scratch, tweaking existing setups, or diving deep into modifier logic, SXM gives you a clean, modern interface to work with Traktor's powerful but notoriously opaque mapping system.

No more hunting through endless menu trees or fighting with Traktor's built-in controller manager. Just open your TSI, see everything at a glance, and make your changes.

Inspired by the much loved, long dead, Xtreme Mapping.

The Story

"

We used to love Xtreme Mapping. It was the only decent TSI editor out there, and it made creating custom Traktor mappings almost enjoyable.

Then Apple Silicon happened, and Xtreme Mapping stopped working. Our attempts to contact the original author, Vincenzo Pietropaolo, were met with silence. After years of waiting and hoping, we decided to take matters into our own hands.

Using Claude Code, we reverse-engineered a completely new TSI editor from a combination of Xtreme Mapping screenshots, the CMDR editor source code (thank you, cmdr-editor team!), and IvanZ's invaluable TSI format research. The result is Super Xtreme Mapping: a love letter to the original, rebuilt from the ground up for modern Macs.
